## Quick Start

1. Download `Whistype-1.0.0.dmg` from [Releases](https://github.com/wasxxm/Whistype/releases/latest)
2. Drag Whistype to `/Applications`
3. Launch and complete onboarding (microphone + accessibility permissions)
4. Hold **⌥ Space** anywhere to dictate
