Features

Everything happens
on your machine.

No servers. No latency. No privacy concerns. Just you and your Mac.

Push-to-Talk

Hold the hotkey to record, release to transcribe. Natural as a walkie-talkie.

Auto-Paste

Text lands directly in whatever app you're using. No extra steps.

Fully Private

Every model runs on Apple Silicon. Zero network calls. Your audio is never uploaded.

Transcription History

Past transcriptions saved locally. Browse and copy anytime from the menu bar.

Menu Bar Native

Lives in your menu bar. No dock icon, no window clutter. Always one hotkey away.

Multiple Engines

WhisperKit, Parakeet, Qwen3-ASR. Pick the model that fits your workflow.

How It Works

Three seconds from
voice to text.

No signup. No setup wizard. Install and go.

01

Install

Download the .dmg, drag to Applications. The speech model downloads on first launch.

02

Hold & speak

Press ⌥ + Space anywhere. A floating capsule appears while you talk.

03

Release

Your words are transcribed on-device and pasted into the active app instantly.
